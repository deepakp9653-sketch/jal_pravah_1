🌧️ 2021 — Record Breaking Year
Jun–Sep 2021 · 67 rainy days · Peak: 122.8mm in single day
+79% above normal
Yamuna breached warning level in August. Extensive flooding in low-lying areas near Okhla, Yamuna Bank. 3rd highest seasonal rainfall since 1901.
💥 2024 — Large Excess Year
Jun–Sep 2024 · 58 rainy days · Peak: 99.2mm in single day
+61% — 7th highest since 1901
Yamuna reached highest level since 2013. Over 10,000 residents evacuated from low-lying areas. ITO, Minto Bridge severely flooded. Flood Control Centre activated.
⚡ 2023 — Intense Short Bursts
Jun–Sep 2023 · 48 rainy days · Extreme event: 153mm on July 9
Near normal (±2%)
July 9 cloudburst event (153mm/day) caused major flooding. Red Fort, ITO, Kashmiri Gate, Civil Lines submerged. Yamuna at 208.6m — alert level crossed.
